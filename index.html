ABOUT ME

I am a thorough and detail-oriented former hair stylist currently transitioning into development. I went through a Javascript boot camp through Eleven Fifty Academy founded by Scott Jones where I studied vanilla JS, React, Node.js and Angular. Before getting my certification, I was introduced to graphic design at my previous job as an Administrative Assitant at an elementary school. I developed an urge to create and was introduced to coding through Indy Women in Tech, an organization that is bridging the gender gap in the tech community by getting women involved in STEM, and who graciously provided me with a scholarship to get certified.

I went to cosmetology school after high school and was a hair stylist for 5 years where I always enjoyed meeting and getting to know new people. Originally from Indiana, I relocated to Los Angeles two years ago after the physical demands of working behind the chair became too much and I decided to switch careers. I am a passionate person, always looking to push myself to be a better developer with the goal of creating applications that are both streamlined and beautiful.

You can find my code on Github, and learn more
about me on my LinkedIn and resume.
