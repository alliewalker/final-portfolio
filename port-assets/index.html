ABOUT ME

I am a thorough and detail oriented problem solver currently transitioning from cosmetology to development. Before getting my certification at Eleven Fifty Academy in Indiana, I worked as an Administrative Assitant at an elementary school where I was introducted to graphic design. I developed an urge to create and was introduced to the coding world through a friend.

I was a hair stylist for 5 years where I always enjoyed meeting and getting to know new people. I am originally from Indiana, but relocated to Mar Vista, California two years ago. I did hair for the first six months, but decided that I wanted to do something bigger than being behind the chair. I am a passionate person, always looking to push myself to be a better developer with the goal to create applications that are both streamlined and beautiful to provide elegant solutions for clients.

You can find my code on Github, and learn more about me on LinkedIn and resume.
